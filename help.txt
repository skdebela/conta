Conta Contact Manager Usage Manual

Usage:
  ./main.sh [options]

Options:
  -A, --add          Add a new contact
                     Example: ./main.sh -A -n "Manuel" -p "Mobile=012345678,Work=198765434" -e "Personal=email@host.domain" -c "Friend,Family"
                     
  -C, --categorize   Categorize contacts by a specific category
                     Example: ./main.sh -C "Friends"

  -S, --search       Search for a contact by name, phone, or email
                     Example: ./main.sh -S "Manuel"
  
  -V, --view         View all contacts
                     Example: ./main.sh -V
                     

  -n, --name         Name of the contact (used with -A)
  -p, --phones       Phone numbers (used with -A)
  -e, --emails       Emails (used with -A)
  -c, --categories   Categories (used with -A)
  -h, --help         Display this usage manual
